Simple Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coca powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

    • Frosting:

  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Instructions:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.

    Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

    Add in the egg, milk, oil and vanilla and mix very well.

    Slowly pour the boiling water and gently stir-batter. (It will become very thin)

    Pour batter in the dish and put in the oven. Bake for about 35-40 minutes or until inserted toothpick is clean.

    Take out and let cool. (You can stop here or continue with the frosting)

    Melt the chocolate chips and mix with sour cream and the vanilla and spread on the bread.

    Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups melted unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg & 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsps baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
  • (Optional) 1 cup milk chocolate chips

    • Instructions

    Mix the melted butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar.

    Add the egg, yolk, vanilla and mix well.

    Mix in the chocolate chips and leave for 20 minutes.

    Turn oven to 325 degrees

    Grease the pan and shape then batter into small balls.

    Bake for about 10 minutes

    Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1 can 100% pure pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsps baking powder
  • 1 tsp milk
  • 2 tsps cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

    • Instructions

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

    Combine oil, pumpkin, sugar, egg and vanilla.

    Separate bowl, combine baking soda and milk. Then add to pumpkin mixture.

    Separate bowl, combine cinnamon, baking poweder, salt and flour. Then add to pumkin mixture.

    NOTE: if gooey, just add more flour.

    Fold in chocolate chips.

    Allow batter to sit for 15 minutes.

    Grease pan and shape cookies and put them on cookie sheet.

    Bake for 10-15 minutes and ENJOY!

    Banana Bread

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 bananas, finely crushed (4 for a more mois bread)
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla (opt)

    • Instructions

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

    Cream together butter and sugar

    Add eggs and crushed bananas and combine all four ingredients well.

    sift together flour, soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Add vanilla.

    Mix just until combined, DO NOT overmix.

    Pour into greased and floured loaf pan

    Bake for 55 minutes

    ENJOY!
